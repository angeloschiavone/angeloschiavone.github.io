Independent house in a quiet district, just a few minutes from the city center

The house dates back to 1930, rebuilt after the damage caused by the bombings of the Second World War that hit Milan.

In 2014 Mimmo and his sons carefully renovated the entire building and customized the spaces. The house has two independent floors and a pretty courtyard garden, where you can enjoy breakfast in the summer mornings. In winter time breakfast will be served in the common room where also cold and hot drinks will be available for free the whole day.

Da Canal B&B is located in the heart of Milan Affori, an ancient duchy in the northern part of the city. A short walk from the B&B you will find Villa Litta and its hilly park, a former summer residence of the Milanese nobility, now home of the Municipal Library.

We are located just 300 meters from the subway station Affori Centro (yellow line): in a few subway stops you will admire the majesty of the Duomo cathedral or stroll along the "Galleria Vittorio Emanuele", reach the "Teatro alla Scala" and the "Castello Sforzesco". The North Park, with its 1500 acres of nature, is just a few steps away. Exploring the North Park you can find more than 20 km of routes, some of them dive into the province of Milan, reaching the towns of Cinisello and Bresso. Rho-Pero Milan Fair is easily reachable by subway or by car.

Da Canal B&B is also a great starting point to reach Como and the northern lakes, in fact we are just a few steps from FNM railway station, from where regional trains depart. We are also near "Isola" district with its bars and restaurants and the new "Porta Nuova" district, with its modern buildings.
