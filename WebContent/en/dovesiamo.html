How to get here

Watch the video to see how to get here from "Affori centro" subway station (yellow line)

We are just a few steps away from Affori Centro metro station (M3 yellow line), in via Bernardo Da Canal 24.


How to reach us by plane:

From Malpensa airport there are 2 possibilities:

  1. take shuttle bus to Milan Central Station (Stazione Centrale FS) then subay linea 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.
  2. Malpensa express train to "Stazione Cadorna FNM", then subway line 1 (the red one) to “Duomo” where you will change to line 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.

From Linate airport there are 2 possibilities:

  1. take shuttle bus to Milan Central Station (Stazione Centrale FS) then subay linea 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.
  2. take urban bus number 73 or 73x to the last stop “San Babila” then subway line 1 (the red one) to “Duomo” where you will change to line 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.

From Orio al Serio airport: take shuttle bus to Milan Central Station (Stazione Centrale FS) then subay linea 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.


How to get here by train:

From Milan Central Station (Stazione Centrale FS): take subay line 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.


From Porta Garibaldi station there are 2 possibilities:

  1. take subway line 2 (the green one) to “Stazione Centrale FS” (2 stops, direction "Cologno") then change to subay line 3 (the yellow one) 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.
  2. bus n° 70 (take it in Via Farini – Via Ferrari) to Biancamano – Cim. Bruzzano) 10 stops, get off at Via P. Rossi – Via Armellini (200mt from Da Canal B&B)

From Cadorna Station FNM there are 2 possibilities:

  1. subway line 1 (the red one) to “Duomo” where you will change to line 3 (the yellow one), 5 stops in Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.
  2. Take suburban train "Trenord" line S2 or S4 get off at “Stazione FNM Affori”.

From Rogoredo FS station: take line 3 (the yellow one), Comasina direction, get off at Affori Centro station.
